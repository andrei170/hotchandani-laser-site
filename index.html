Our Story

Built on 35 years of physician-led expertise

Dr. Gope Hotchandani, MD, opened this practice in Green Bay in 1991 with a conviction that aesthetic medicine belongs in the hands of a trained clinician, full stop. As a board-certified general surgeon and former Chief of Surgery at the VA Medical Center in Iron Mountain, Michigan, he brought surgical precision to a category that was still finding its footing.

  • Board-certified general surgeon, general surgery residency at New Rochelle Hospital Medical Center
  • Former Chief of Surgery, VA Medical Center, Iron Mountain, Michigan
  • Aesthetic and laser specialization since 1997, the earliest in Northeast Wisconsin
  • AAAS Award of Excellence in Aesthetics Education
  • Allergan Top 10 Injector recognition

A second generation

Timothy Hotchandani carries the practice forward as the second generation, managing outward presence and helping introduce the next chapter of treatments to a patient base that, in many cases, has been coming to this clinic for decades. In a category full of newcomers, that continuity is the rarest credential of all.
